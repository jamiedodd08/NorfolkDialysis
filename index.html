Welcome to Norfolk Holiday Dialysis

We are a small, friendly, family-run dialysis clinic where you can dialyse in a relaxed and quiet environment with TV, free wi-fi, and refreshments, in the knowledge that your treatment is safe in the hands of our very experienced nurses.

Norfolk Holiday Dialysis is a family-run holiday dialysis clinic. We have 15 years experience in dialysis between us. We offer an experienced and professional approach, but in the peace and quiet of our friendly, relaxed clinic.

Treatment costs are now paid directly through NHS England. Your home unit no longer has to pay for your dialysis.

Norfolk Holiday Dialysis is completely separate from The Mermaid Centre at Wells Cottage Hospital, and we are not associated with them.

Our clinic is based in the village of Dersingham, just round the corner from Sandringham, and 15 minutes from the seaside town of Hunstanton to the North, and the historic port town of King’s Lynn to the South. The rest of North Norfolk’s beautiful coastline is easily accessible via the coast road which passes the village.
